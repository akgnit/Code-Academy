HTML files also allow you to add comments to your code. Comments begin with . Any characters in between will be treated as a comment. Including comments in your code is helpful for many reasons: They help you (and others) understand your code if you decide to come back and review it at a much later date. They allow you to experiment with new code, without having to delete old code.
